Humanities at Mass Academy is taught by Ms. Small. In Humanities, over the summer, we read Walden by Henry David Thoreau. We wrote and performed our own skits based on the book in front of the whole class.

Now, we are reading the short stories "August 2026: There Will Come Soft Rains" and "The Pedestrian" by Ray Bradbury. Both stories are dystopian pieces that serve to warn people about the potential dangers of an overdependence on technology.
