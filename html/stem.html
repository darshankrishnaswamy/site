STEM

STEM at Mass Academy is taught by Dr. Crowthers. In STEM, we are learning how to make a science project to compete in the local science fair. We have learned about the engineering design process and how to effectively brainstorm ides. We also learn how to develop strategies for troubleshooting when things go wrong with out projects.

My STEM project idea is a fire extinguisher that can automatically detect house fires and extinguish them. It will use a new fire extinguishing technology which uses low-frequency sound waves to put out fires rather than water of chemicals. The automatic sprinklers installed in some houses are only effective against certain types of fire, because water cannot put out oil or electrical fires. This new sound technology works on all types of fires, so it would be able to put out those fires.
