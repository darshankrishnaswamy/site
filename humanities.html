At the beginning of the year, we read the short stories "August 2026: There Will Come Soft Rains" and "The Pedestrian" by Ray Bradbury. Both stories are dystopian pieces that serve to warn people about the potential dangers of an overdependence on technology, but used very different techniques and methods to spread their messages.
