Physics

Physics at Mass Academy is taught by Mr. Ellis. So far, we have learned both 1-dimensional and 2-dimensional kinematics (the study of objects in motion), Forces, Work and Energy, and Rotational Motion concepts.

We sometimes get a single advanced problem, called an Über Problem. These problems have multiple parts and we get about a week to solve the problems and do a full write-up. They are very similar to lab write-ups, but we don't do the experiments ourselves.
